    Why My Face Looks Like it has Freckles

    You might be wondering, does Chesney have freckles? Nope. In November 2019, I was diagnosed with lupus. Bet you didn't know dogs could get lupus! If you don't know what lupus is, lupus is a serious autoimmunde disease that causes my immune system to attack itself. The marks on my face are from scratching when I was younger, but I'm much better now, thanks to my dermatologist.

    Facts about Lupus in Dogs

    I have Discoid Lupus Erythematosus (DLE), which is non-life threatening

    Lupus is usually caused by genetic factors, but can also be worsened by environment factors (like the sun)

    Treatment for DLE is medication so I can live a happy, healthy life
